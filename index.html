Rules :

1. In the game of blackjack, the main objective is to achieve a total value of cards equal to 21 in order to win.
2. If your card total is below 21, you have the option to draw additional cards to increase your total.
3. However, if your total exceeds 21, you will be eliminated from the game and lose.
